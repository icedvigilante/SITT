Origin Scripts

There is an issue with EA's Origin, where Origin will steal focus from your game (which can cause all kinds of issues).
This is caused by an issue within specific files within Origin.
These scripts remove the files (so far, no negative effect) and launch Origin, thus preventing the issue.
